Lógica:

- Busca en project_vendors de la misma

- Excluye vendors del proyecto actual 
  (projectId del param)
- Agrupa por name + type + specialty + company
- Ordena por frecuencia DESC (COUNT)
- Devuelve máximo 10 resultados únicos
- Cada resultado incluye:
  { name, type, specialty, company, 
    email, phone, contract_notes,
    linked_user_id, frequency }

Query aproximada:
SELECT name, type, specialty, company,
  email, phone, contract_notes, 
  linked_user_id,
  COUNT(*) as frequency
FROM project_vendors
WHERE organization_id = :orgId
  AND project_id != :projectId
  AND status != 'cancelled'
GROUP BY name, type, specialty, company,
  email, phone, contract_notes, linked_user_id
ORDER BY frequency DESC
LIMIT 10
