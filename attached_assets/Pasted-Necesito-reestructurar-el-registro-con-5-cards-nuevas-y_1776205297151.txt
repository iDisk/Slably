Cuando role = "client" (Home Owner):
  Mostrar: name, email, password, 
  phone (opcional)
  Ocultar: companyName, state, 
  serviceCity, category

Cuando role = "accountant" (Tax Pro):
  Mostrar: name, email, password,
  firmName (opcional), phone (opcional)
  Ocultar: companyName, state, serviceCity

Cuando role = "realtor":
  Mostrar: name, email, password,
  brokerageName (opcional) 
    placeholder: "RE/MAX, Keller Williams..."
  licenseNumber (opcional)
    placeholder: "License # (optional)"
  city, state
  Ocultar: serviceCity, category

Cuando role = "supplier":
  Mostrar: name, email, password,
  companyName, state, phone
  Ocultar: serviceCity, category

Cuando role = "builder" o "subcontractor" (Pro):
  Mostrar: name, email, password,
  companyName (opcional), 
  serviceCity (required), state, phone
  
PASO 5 — Lógica de plan al registrar

En artifacts/api-server/src/routes/auth.ts
en POST /api/auth/register:

Agrega esta lógica al crear la organización:

const isPaidRole = 
  role === "builder" || 
  role === "subcontractor" || 
  role === "supplier";

const plan = isPaidRole ? "trial" : "free";

Al insertar la organización agrega:
plan: plan

Los roles client, accountant, realtor
quedan con plan: "free" permanentemente.
Los roles builder, subcontractor, supplier
quedan con plan: "trial" inicialmente.
