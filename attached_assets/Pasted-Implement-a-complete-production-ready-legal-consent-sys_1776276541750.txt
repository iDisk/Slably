-------------------------------------

Add fields:

- termsAcceptedAt (timestamp, nullable initially)
- termsVersion (string, e.g., "v1.0")

-------------------------------------

In the registration endpoint (auth.ts or equivalent):

  - Save:
    termsAcceptedAt = current timestamp
    termsVersion = "v1.0"
